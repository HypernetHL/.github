### What is Hypernet

Hypernet is a trading terminal built on Hyperliquid that brings market data,
analytics, and copytrading together in one interface. Instead of switching
between charts, position tracking, and manual order entry, everything lives
in a single real-time view.

### Trade

Live market charts and order flow, with the analytics you need to make a
decision in front of you: positions, funding, and performance, updated in
real time.

### Copytrade

Follow and mirror the activity of other traders on Hyperliquid, so a strategy
you trust runs alongside your own without manual effort.

### Insights

Cut through the noise. Hypernet surfaces the signals that matter, market
movement, funding shifts, and account performance, in a clean, readable
layout built for fast decisions.
